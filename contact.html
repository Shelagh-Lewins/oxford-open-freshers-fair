New society listing

To add your society to this website, please submit the society registration form.

Listing your society on the Oxford Open Freshers' Fair website is free but we ask that you make a donation to a non-political registered charity such as those listed by GiveWell: https://www.givewell.org/

We'll also require your society logo, which should be a square .png or .jpg file not more than 1 MB in size, and a receipt for your charitable donation.

Please send us your logo and receipt by email.

Update your society listing

To update your society's listing, please submit the society update form.

We will ask you to confirm your society's name and contact email address before making any updates.

To change your society logo, please send it to us by email. Your logo should be a square .png or .jpg file not more than 1 MB in size.
