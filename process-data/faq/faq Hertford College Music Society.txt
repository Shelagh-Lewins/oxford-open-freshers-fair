Q: Do you have to be a student at Hertford to take part in our ensembles? 
A: No our ensembles are open to all students!

Q: What ensembles do you have?
A: We have a Chapel Choir, Orchestra, and Jazz Band. We also hold weekly recitals.

Q: Do you have to be a certain standard to take part?
A: No! We are proudly non-auditioning and so welcome people of all capabilities. We have a real mix of players and singers from diploma standard to beginner.
