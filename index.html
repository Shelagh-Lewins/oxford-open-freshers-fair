Welcome to Oxford Open Freshers' Fair. Our 2020 Freshers' Fair was complementary to the Student Union's fair. The SU was unable to accommodate many societies, so we created this website to be an inclusive online space for student societies and organisations.

Click on a society's image to find out more about it, frequently asked questions and how to get involved.

Freshers' Week 2020 is over, but this site remains active partly to help provide information to students during the year - most societies will welcome new members at any time - and also in anticipation of Freshers' Week 2021, when an Open Freshers Fair may again be of value.

If you want to add your society to this page, or update your society listing, please contact us.
